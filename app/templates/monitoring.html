Monitoring Options

Level 1 Monitoring:

This feature enables you to monitor the solar production only. To enable this feature, it is essential to have an internet connection at your property. Our team provides connection of the inverter to your WiFi, however if this is not available at the inverter location, then one of the following can be used:

  • LAN cable connection - $150
  • 4G Modem - $250 + data plan
  • Wi-Fi Extender - $100
Level 2 Monitoring:

This monitoring feature enables you to monitor your solar production, your electricity consumption and your grid export/import. This feature is best if you wish to monitor your daily usage and to maximise the benefits of the available solar system. This option is also required in some cases where export limitation is required by the supply authority. Please contact us to discuss this feature or for more information.
