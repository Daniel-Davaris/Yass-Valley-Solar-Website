Our Team

From a young age, Growing up and working in an local electrical business, Tim developed a particular fascination with solar and renewable energy. This planted a seed which grew and in 2013 Tim founded Ygrene Energy (now Yass Valley Solar) with the vision of providing the same honest and reliable service to the solar industry that the family electrical business had been providing to their customers for many years.

Tim focused on providing the best service possible and found a particular niche in off-grid power systems, were he found significant room for improvement for the traditional installations that were being installed while also continuing to provide commercial and residential solar systems in the area.

Today Yass Valley Solar is a thriving business completing a wide array of solar projects and covering a multitude of work scopes. Throughout the evolution of the company, one thing has remained constant - the passion and commitment that each team member has to provide the same honest and reliable service that was instilled into our company culture many years ago.
